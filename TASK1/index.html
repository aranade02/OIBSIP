Most famous Destinations

From religious to historical come to see the best of India.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is located on the right bank of the Yamuna River in a vast Mughal garden, in the Agra District in Uttar Pradesh.

Golden Temple

The Golden temple is located in the holy city of the Sikhs, Amritsar. The Golden temple is famous for the sacred pilgrim spots for Sikhs.

Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is situated in Mumbai, and is located at Apollo Bunder in South Mumbai, on the shore of the Arabian Sea.

Festivals

Enjoy the festivals and feasts

Diwali

Deepavali or Diwali is an Indian festival of lights. ‘Deepa’ means lamp or light and‘Vali’ means string or row, and Deepavali means rows of lights.It celebrates the victory of good over the evil.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Among all festivals, Ganesh Chaturthi holds a special significance for Hindus. The festival commemorates the birth of the much-loved Hindu deity, Lord Ganesha which spans over ten days mark the celebrations.

Holi

Holi is considered as festival of love and the festival of colors, it signifies the arrival of spring and the victory of good over evil. It is celebrated all over the country with a lot of enthusiasm and fervor.
